Simple Black Jack

How To Play This Game

This is simple Black Jack game. Get total value of all cards close to 21 as possible without exceeding it. Number cards are worth their face value, face cards (kings, queens, and jacks) are each worth 10 points, and aces can be 1 or 11 points. If your hand is closer to 21 than the dealer's without going over, you win! If your hand exceeds 21, you lose.
